About Swiss Bank of India

Who We Are

Our History

Swiss Bank of India was founded in 1964 with a vision to provide world-class banking services to Indian citizens and businesses. With over 60 years of innovation and trust, we have grown into one of India's leading financial institutions.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is to empower individuals and businesses through financial solutions that foster growth, security, and innovation. Our core values include transparency, trust, customer satisfaction, and corporate responsibility.

Leadership

Swiss Bank of India is led by an experienced team of professionals who are committed to driving the bank's success and ensuring we remain at the forefront of the financial industry. Our leadership team brings a wealth of experience from various sectors.

Corporate Responsibility

We believe in giving back to the community through initiatives that focus on sustainability, education, and financial literacy. Swiss Bank of India takes pride in being a socially responsible corporate entity.
